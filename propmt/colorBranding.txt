1. Are Orange & Black a Good Brand Combination?

Yes — when done correctly.

What Orange Communicates

Energy

Action

Warmth

Service & friendliness

Great for call-to-action buttons

What Black Communicates

Professionalism

Authority

Cleanliness (important for a cleaning brand)

Works very well for text and structure

👉 For a cleaning services business, orange + black works very well because:

Orange = active service

Black = trust & professionalism

⚠️ The mistake to avoid:
Too much black + too much bright orange = harsh and heavy UI

2. Recommended Brand Color System (Best Practice)

Instead of “orange & black only”, think in layers:

🔶 Primary Brand Color (Hero Color)

Use this for:

Logo accents

Primary buttons

Active states

Highlights

Recommended Orange Options

Burnt Orange → #F28C28

Warm Orange → #F7931E

Soft Amber → #FF9F1C

👉 Avoid neon orange. Go slightly muted.

⚫ Secondary Color (Structure & Text)

Use for:

Headings

Navbar

Footer

Icons

Recommended Black Variants

Charcoal Black → #1C1C1C

Soft Black → #222222

👉 Avoid pure black #000000 — it’s too harsh on the eyes.

⚪ Neutral Colors (VERY IMPORTANT)

These make the site feel clean and premium.

Off-white background → #F9FAFB

Light gray → #E5E7EB

Medium gray text → #6B7280

These are what give you:

White space

Breathing room

Professional feel

🟦 Optional Accent Color (Recommended)

This elevates the brand.

Best Accent Choices with Orange & Black

Deep Navy → #0F172A

Teal → #0EA5A4

Soft Blue → #2563EB

Use accent color for:

Links

Secondary buttons

Icons

Hover states

4. Buttons & UI Usage
Primary Button

Background: Orange

Text: White

Hover: Slightly darker orange

Secondary Button

Background: Transparent

Border: Orange

Text: Orange

Danger / Warning

Dark Orange / Red-orange

Never pure red unless necessary

5. Fonts Recommendation (Very Important)
Gotham fonts, I have it in my root folder under fonts

6. Light Mode vs Dark Mode
Light Mode (Default)

Background: Off-white

Text: Dark charcoal

Buttons: Orange

Cards: White with soft shadow

Dark Mode (Optional)

Background: #0F172A

Text: #E5E7EB

Primary actions: Orange

Cards: Slightly lighter dark gray

⚠️ Orange looks AMAZING in dark mode when used sparingly.

7. Transparency & Effects

Use transparency subtly:

Card shadows: soft, not heavy

Glassmorphism (optional): only for modals

Hover effects: slight scale or color shift

Avoid:

Heavy gradients everywhere

Overusing blur

Too many animations

8. Overall Look & Feel You Should Aim For

Keywords:

Clean

Trustworthy

Professional

Modern service brand

Not playful, not too corporate

Think:

“A company I can trust to clean my space properly.”
