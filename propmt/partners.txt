Partners Section (Trust & Credibility Focus):

Design a clean, minimal Partners section that showcases the companies and organizations we currently work with.

This section’s primary goal is to build trust instantly, not to dominate attention.

🔹 Section Structure

Place this section after the Services section.

Include:

A small, subtle heading above the logos:

Example: “Trusted by leading companies” or “Our Partners”

A horizontal row of partner logos underneath.

🧱 Logo Layout

Display partner logos in a single horizontal row.

Logos should be:

Evenly spaced

Vertically aligned

Consistent in visual weight

Use a flex or grid layout that automatically adjusts spacing.

🎨 Visual Treatment

Logos should be:

Monochrome (grayscale) by default

Subtle and non-distracting

On hover:

Transition from grayscale to full color

Smooth, gentle animation (no bounce or scale jump)

📐 Sizing & Spacing

Logos must not appear oversized.

Maintain generous horizontal padding so the section feels airy.

Section background should be:

White or very light neutral

No heavy borders

Optional thin divider line above or below

📱 Responsiveness

Desktop:

Single row of logos

Tablet:

Logos wrap gracefully into two rows if needed

Mobile:

Logos wrap into multiple rows or scroll horizontally

✨ Motion & Polish

Subtle fade-in animation on scroll.

Hover effect only on individual logos.

No autoplay sliders unless necessary.

🧠 Implementation Notes

Use Next.js Image optimization for logos.

Use Tailwind CSS for layout and spacing.

Ensure logos have accessible alt text.

This section should feel:

Quietly confident

Professional

Trust-building

Clean and modern

Do not over-decorate or distract from the logos themselves.
